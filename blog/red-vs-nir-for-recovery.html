Red vs Near‑Infrared for Recovery

2025-08-18 • Wellness • Educational (not medical advice)

Quick Differences

  • Red (630–660 nm): surface and mid-dermis — skin quality, scars, inflammation.
  • NIR (810–850 nm): deeper reach — muscles, joints, nerves, recovery.
  • Tip: Use red for skin-facing sessions; NIR for deeper tissues; combine with sound for relaxation.

Session Basics

  • Distance: 6–12 inches from LEDs.
  • Time: 5–15 min per area (start low, increase slowly).
  • Frequency: 3–5x/week is common; consistency beats intensity.
  • Eyes: Close or shield for bright panels.
