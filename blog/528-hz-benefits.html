528 Hz: Benefits & Limits

2025-08-18 • Wellness • Educational (not medical advice)

What 528 Hz Is (and Isn't)

  • Is: A soothing tone associated with calm, focus, and positive mood.
  • Isn't: A DNA repair tool. There’s no evidence that sound alone repairs DNA.

How to Use

  • 5–10 minutes for grounding or breathwork.
  • Headphones for meditation; speakers for room ambience.
  • Stack with red/NIR sessions to reduce stress while cells get energy.
