Due diligence checklist
  • Confirm wavelength bins (e.g., 660 ±10 nm, 850 ±10 nm) and total LED count.
  • Request measured irradiance at specific distances (mW/cm²) and safety certifications (FCC/CE/ROHS).
  • Check power, thermal management, and duty cycle for wearables.
  • Ask for OEM/ODM options: branding, packaging, manuals.
  • Agree on MOQ, lead time, QC sampling, and warranty terms.
Tip: keep all claims educational and avoid medical promises when listing products.
