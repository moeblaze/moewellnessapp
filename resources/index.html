How to choose

  • Wavelengths: look for 630–660 nm (red) and 810–850 nm (NIR). Optional 1064 nm for deeper targets.
  • Coverage: masks for face, wraps for core/back, braces for joints, panels for whole-body.
  • Irradiance: moderate power with consistent sessions beats ultra-high intensity.
  • Safety: avoid staring into LEDs; start low, go slow.
