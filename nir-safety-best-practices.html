NIR Safety & Best Practices

2025-08-18 • Wellness • Educational (not medical advice)

Safety First

  • Start low, go slow. More isn’t always better.
  • Avoid staring into bright LEDs; use eye shields if needed.
  • Do not use over active malignancies, and consult a professional if pregnant or managing medical conditions.

Dosage Variables

  • Irradiance, distance, angle, and skin tone matter.
  • Shorter sessions more often usually outperform marathon sessions.
